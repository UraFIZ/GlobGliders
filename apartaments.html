Selina NQ1 Manchester / Loft

15 Hilton St, Manchester M1 1JJ, UK

Selina’s most luxurious offer! The Loft is designed for those looking to indulge while traveling. Wake up to expansive views of the city and ease into the bustling energy of this vibrant place. The Loft offers ample space that can be used for a family to comfortably stay or to hold a small event or gathering. have a private bathroom as well as your own space to chill. One of the best parts? You get to be a part of the vibrant, friendly Selina community.

Manchester

Selina NQ1’s recently refurbished Victorian building features a coffee shop, library, fusion restaurant and cocktail bar. Now part of Manchester’s Northern Quarter in St Stephen’s Square, Selina NQ1 is flanked by gastro pubs, vintage shops and concept stories with stylish students and beer-loving crowds at every turn. Our weekly event calendar is packed with evening acoustic gigs, deep house DJ sets and even live drag queen show hosted by local performers!

Need to get some work done? Escape to our cowork area, where you can take your meetings, answer emails and be productive on high-speed WiFi. After catching up on some work, jump on a group tour to the Victoria Baths, Lowry’s waterside art gallery and get a dose of green a Fletcher Moss’s Botanical Gardens. Weekend plans with the family? Kids are more than welcome!
